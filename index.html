About me

I've always believed that good design is experienced, not just seen.

I care about how something works just as much as how it looks. Every spacing decision, every interaction, every word on screen — it all matters. Attention to detail isn't a habit, it's how I think.

My process starts before any screen is designed. I research the problem thoroughly, map user flows, and challenge assumptions early — using Claude to prototype and stress-test ideas, and Figma to bring them to life with precision.

Let's make something worth using.
